7. Jobs that interest me

This part is dedicated to the presentation of the jobs that interest me in the cybersecurity sector.


Working in the world of cybersecurity is above all a continuity in learning that will be done on a daily basis in order to be on the lookout for new developments.

It is therefore essential at some point to undertake specialized training.
The main mission of the cybersecurity expert is to protect the data as well as the reliability of a company's computer system.

To do this, he is required to manage certain missions during his activity:

- Diagnose the company's information system in order to detect possible flaws.

- Propose a range of solutions to secure the company's information and data.

- Put in place the different processes and security charters.

- Ensure the sustainability of security systems.

- Adapt security systems to new threats and changing technologies.
